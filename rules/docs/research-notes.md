# Research notes

- Distinguish facts from assumptions. Use "confirmed:" and "assumed:" prefixes where the distinction matters.
- Include a "references" section if citing papers, internal reports, or data sources. Do not paraphrase a citation as if it were your own finding.
- Date research notes at the top. Research older than 6 months in a fast-moving domain should be re-validated before acting on it.
- Do not include raw data, PII, or proprietary excerpts from confidential sources in research notes committed to the repo.
